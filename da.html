Analysis of Electoral Bond Data

Tools:

Pandas, Matplotlib, Jupyter


In this project I have analyzed Electoral Bonds data published on website of 'Election Commision of India' , and tried to answer some salient questions regarding political funding.

🔑 Key Insights: -
💰 Out of 24 parties, BHARATIYA JANATA PARTY has received the maximum donation through electoral bonds 6060.5 Cr which is ~ 47%.
💰 BJP is followed by TMC and INC who received 1609 Cr (12.60%) and 1422 Cr (11.13%) respectively.
💰 FUTURE GAMING AND HOTEL SERVICES has alone purchased 11.25% bonds worth 1368 Cr.
