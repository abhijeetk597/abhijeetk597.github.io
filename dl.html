Dog Vision

Tools:

TensorFlow, Streamlit


Dog Vision aka. Dog Breed classification is a Deep Learning Project including deployment of the app on Streamlit cloud.

This project is inspired from Kaggle's 'Dog Breed Identification' competition with over 120 breeds and 10k+ images in both training and test data-sets.

'Transfer Learning' has been implemented using two different models `mobilenet_v2` & `resnet_v2_50` from TensorFlow Hub.

SkimLit

Tools:

TensorFlow


SkimLit is an NLP Project to classify Medical Research paper abstract sentences.

Conducted a series of modeling experiments to develop an NLP model to classify abstract sentences into their respective roles (e.g., Background, Objective, Methods, Results, Conclusion), streamlining literature review processes

Reached an impressive accuracy of ~88% with the tribrid model, featuring PubMed BERT Expert alongside token-level embeddings, character-level embeddings, and positional embeddings.
