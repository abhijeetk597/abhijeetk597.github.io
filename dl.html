Dog Vision

Tools:

TensorFlow, Streamlit


Dog Vision aka. Dog Breed classification is a Deep Learning Project including deployment of the app on Streamlit cloud.

This project is inspired from Kaggle's 'Dog Breed Identification' competition with over 120 breeds and 10k+ images in both training and test data-sets.

'Transfer Learning' has been implemented using two different models `mobilenet_v2` & `resnet_v2_50` from TensorFlow Hub.
